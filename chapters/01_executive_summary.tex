\chapter{Executive Summary}

This report documents five machine learning laboratory projects covering \textbf{binary text classification} (18,807 raw emails, 9,886 preprocessed), \textbf{time-series demand forecasting} (1,018,209 sales records), \textbf{customer segmentation} (2,200 customers), \textbf{image color quantization} (108,000 pixels), and \textbf{tabulated regression} (20,640 California housing records). Each project implements core algorithms \textit{from scratch} in pure NumPy and benchmarks them against scikit-learn, revealing the inner mechanics of every model.
